About the application.

Description:
The purpose of the application is to aid individuals/organizations in organizing a garage sale.

List of Features:
Before a sale:
  • Create a garage sale
  • List items
  • Print tags
  • Share/advertise on social media
  • Assign roles to other organizers
  • Search of an item or sale
  • Bidding and purchasing of items on-line.
During a sale:
  • Create transaction
  • Email receipt
After a sale:
  • Deactivate sale
  • View financial reports
Others:
  • Special privileges for superuser, administrator, seller, cashier, and bookkeeper.
  • Registration
  • Log In
  • Lock / Unlock users
